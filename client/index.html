Who is Bison?

Bison is a privately held, professionally managed transportation company, established in 1969. With a network of terminals, warehouses, and yards throughout Canada, the U.S. and Mexico, Bison is a leading asset-based freight solutions provider that employs over 4,000 professional drivers and staff. We proudly deliver award-winning transportation services to our valued clients throughout North America. We operate one of the largest, safest, and most modern fleets on the road today with an industry-low claims ratio of less than 0.1%. Our ongoing investments in tractor, trailer, and container equipment is a testament to our commitment.
